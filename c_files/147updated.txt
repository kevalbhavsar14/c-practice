 computer is  machine that can be instructed to carry out sequences of arithmetic or logical operations automatically via computer programming. Modern computers have  ability to follow generalized sets of operations, called programs. These programs enable computers to perform  extremely wide range of tasks.  "complete" computer including  hardware,  operating system (main software), and peripheral equipment required and used for "full" operation can be referred to as  computer system. This term may as well be used for  group of computers that are connected and work together, in particular  computer network or computer cluster. Computers are used as control systems for  wide variety of industrial and consumer devices. This includes simple special-purpose devices like microwave ovens and remote controls, factory devices such as industrial robots and computer-aided design, and also general-purpose devices like personal computers and mobile devices such as smartphones.  Internet is run on computers and it connects hundreds of millions of other computers and their users. users.
